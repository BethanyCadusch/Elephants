What is the African Bush Elephant?

The African Bush Elephant or the scientific name Loxodonta africana is also known as the African Savanna Elephant . The Bush Elephant lives in the central and southern areas of Africa, which is the same as its relative, the Forest Elephant. Another similarity is the life span of 60-70 years. The most distinctive feature of the African Bush Elephant is its large round ears, and it is the largest elephant out of all three existing species. They can weigh an average of 3,600kg - 5,400kg.

Fun Fact

The African Bush Elephant can drink up to 50 gallons of water!
